**Key Concepts Explained Simply**

1. **Trading vs. Investing**: Trading involves short-term strategies to maximize returns daily, monthly, or quarterly, whereas investing takes a long-term approach to the markets.
2. **Round Lots**: Stocks typically trade in round lots, or orders of at least 100 shares.
3. **Options and Futures**: These trade by the contract, with one contract representing some unit of the underlying security.
4. **Bid and Ask**: The prices of shares on a stock market are set through an auction process where buyers and sellers place bids and offers to buy or sell.
5. **Options Strategy**: Options allow for potential profit during volatile times, taking advantage of market conditions to profit while taking on the risk of losing money.

**Practical Trading Tips**

1. **Start with a solid understanding of trading and investing**: Understand the difference between trading and investing, and choose the approach that suits your goals and risk tolerance.
2. **Choose a reputable broker**: Select a broker that fits your trading style and provides excellent apps for analyzing indicators and executing trades.
3. **Develop a trading strategy**: Create a plan that takes into account your risk management practices, capital available, and market conditions.
4. **Diversify your portfolio**: Spread your investments across different asset classes to minimize risk.
5. **Monitor and adjust**: Continuously monitor your trades and adjust your strategy as needed to maximize returns.
