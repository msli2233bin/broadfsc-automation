**Social Media Strategies:**

### Platform-Specific Strategies

#### TikTok

* Utilize the "Add to Music App" feature to drive music discovery and conversion to streaming success.
* Leverage the power of user-generated content (UGC) to create trends and drive engagement.
* Partner with emerging artists to create exclusive content and promote their music.

#### Other Platforms

* Consider implementing similar music discovery features on other platforms, such as Spotify or Apple Music.
* Utilize UGC and influencer marketing to drive engagement and promote music on other platforms.

### Content Creation Tips

#### Music-Related Content

* Create music-related challenges or trends to drive engagement and encourage users to share their own content.
* Partner with artists to create exclusive content, such as behind-the-scenes footage or live performances.
* Utilize music-related hashtags to increase discoverability and reach a wider audience.

#### User-Generated Content

* Encourage users to share their own music-related content, such as covers or remixes.
* Utilize UGC to create a sense of community and encourage users to engage with each other's content.
* Feature user-generated content on the platform's main page or in a dedicated section.

### Engagement Tactics

#### Community Building

* Create a community around music-related content, such as a dedicated music section or a music-themed challenge.
* Encourage users to interact with each other's content and engage in discussions about music.
* Utilize social media listening to stay on top of music-related conversations and trends.

#### Influencer Marketing

* Partner with influencers in the music industry to promote music and drive engagement.
* Utilize influencer marketing to reach a wider audience and increase brand awareness.
* Encourage influencers to share their own music-related content and engage with the platform's community.

### Algorithm Insights

#### Music Discovery

* The "Add to Music App" feature is a key driver of music discovery on TikTok, with over 6 billion track saves in the past year.
* Music-related content is a key driver of engagement on TikTok, with users spending more time watching music-related videos than any other type of content.
* The platform's algorithm prioritizes music-related content, making it more likely to appear in users' feeds.

#### User Behavior

* Users are more likely to engage with music-related content that is relevant to their interests and preferences.
* Users are more likely to share music-related content that is creative and engaging.
* Users are more likely to interact with music-related content that is interactive, such as challenges or quizzes.
